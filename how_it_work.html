Transafty aims to build a trusted community marketplace for people to list, discover, and book trucks and construction equipment.

We have simplified the process into 3 simple steps so that you could Book a truck or construction equipment in 60 seconds and schedule your cargo delivery service for as fast as 5 minutes or less

  2. The amount is invoiced to you. After payment is received, the booking will be confirmed
  3. Receive vendor details and updates through email notifications
